Problems

That are causing deforestation
Industrial Agriculture

Industrial agriculture is one of the primary drivers of deforestation worldwide. Large swathes of forest land are often cleared to make way for agricultural activities

Resource Mining

Large areas of forests are often cleared to extract minerals and other resources. Resource mining is a large contributor to global deforestation.

Urban Expansion

As human population grows, the demand for land to build houses for people also grows. This often leads to deforestation as forests are cleared to provide for new residential areas.

Plant with Purpose

Plant a Tree!

The most straightforward solution to deforestation is to plant a tree. You can consider it a live-long investment in contributing in combating deforestation. Trees absorb carbon-dioxide and prevent landslides and rock slides. OR you can just donate to non-profit organizations and they will plant the trees for you.

Invenitmundo

Use Less Paper

Reduce paper consumption by using the digital counterpart of certain document or tool. For example, you can help reduce paper consumption by using emails and write and send documents online. If you do require paper, choose a recycled paper to minimize the demand for fresh wood pulp.

Treehugger

Recycle

Recycle paper and cardboard to extend their lifecycle and reduce the need for new materials. You can do this by donating your paper waste to a waste collector organization. Your paper waste will be transported to a recycling facility. Opt in for recycled products, expecially paper.
